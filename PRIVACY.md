# Privacy Policy — DDL Radar

_Last updated: 2026-07-05_

DDL Radar (the website, PWA, and the Chrome/Edge browser extension) does **not**
collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

- **No accounts.** There is nothing to sign up for.
- **No analytics or tracking.** No third-party scripts, no cookies, no telemetry.
- **The only network request** the extension makes is fetching the public,
  read-only conference dataset from
  `https://yzyhhhstudy.github.io/paper-deadlines/data.json`. Nothing is sent
  along with it beyond a normal HTTP request.
- **All personal state stays on your device.** Starred conferences, board
  status, language/theme preferences and the cached dataset live in your
  browser's local storage (`localStorage` / `chrome.storage.local`) and never
  leave your machine.

The project is fully open source:
https://github.com/YzYhhhstudy/paper-deadlines

## 中文说明

DDL Radar（网页 / PWA / 浏览器插件）不收集、不存储、不传输任何个人数据。
无账号、无埋点、无第三方脚本。插件唯一的网络请求是拉取公开只读的会议数据
